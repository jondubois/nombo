This is a basic (and unpractical) memo app. You may want to open this app in another tab to see how nCombo synchronizes sessions across all open tabs.
Close all tabs and leave them closed for 5 seconds to destroy the session and all related data.

Once you add a note to the table below, you can share it with the chat app ( http://ncombo.com:8000 ). nCombo allows you to do this with a single line of code.
